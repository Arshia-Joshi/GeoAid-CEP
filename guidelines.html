Before a Disaster

  • Create a family emergency plan.
  • Build a disaster supply kit with water, food, and first aid.
  • Know evacuation routes and local emergency contacts.

During a Disaster

  • Stay calm and follow instructions from authorities.
  • Use battery-powered radio for updates if power is out.
  • Seek shelter or evacuate if advised.

After a Disaster

  • Check yourself and others for injuries.
  • Avoid downed power lines or flooded roads.
  • Stay informed and only return when officials declare it safe.
